Teriyaki Chicken with Rice

Ingredients:

  1. 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cubed
  2. 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  3. 1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce
  4. 1/2 cup water
  5. 3 Tbsp. packed light brown sugar
  6. 2 Tbsp. rice vinegar
  7. 1/2 tsp. sesame oil (optional)
  8. 2 tsp. ground ginger
  9. 2 tsp. minced garlic
  10. 2 Tbsp. honey
  11. 3 tsp. cornstarch
  12. sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish
